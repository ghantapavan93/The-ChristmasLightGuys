Who we are

The Austin Team Behind the Lights

The Christmas Light Guys are a locally owned, fully bonded and insured Austin team who do one thing exceptionally well: take the entire holiday-lighting headache off your plate.

We design a display around your home, install it with commercial-grade lights, service it all season long, then take everything down and store it for next year. No ladders. No tangled boxes in the attic. No burned-out strands to fix yourself — for homes and businesses across Central Texas.

We use professional-grade materials — C-9 bulbs, heavy-gauge high-copper wire, energy-efficient LEDs — with every connection taped and sealed for a clean, custom-fit look that's built to last the season.
